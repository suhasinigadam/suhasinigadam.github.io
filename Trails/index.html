Your Very Own Movie Trailer Maker

Find Out More

Do you love movies?

Would you like to have fun?


Trails is a web application that has a very intuitive and game-like interface that assists movie lovers in building their very own movie trailers. Trails is a tool with which you can have loads of fun by unleashing your creativity and building an awesome movie trailer. You can also cast your friends, tag them to the trailer and also share your creativity with others.
